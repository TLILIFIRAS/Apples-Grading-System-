What it does

Everything you need to grade apples

From raw image to detailed quality report — fully automated.

Precise Detection

Fine-tuned YOLO26 model trained on a Roboflow apple dataset. Detects multiple apples per image with bounding boxes and confidence scores.

Disease Classification

ResNet18 classifier identifies 4 conditions per apple crop: HEALTHY, BLOTCH, ROT, and SCAB with full probability scores.

Batch Processing

Analyze up to 20 images in a single request. Batch crops are stacked into one tensor for efficient parallel inference.

Color Analysis

HSV-based redness scoring and hue uniformity measurement. Apples are rated good, fair, or poor for color quality.

PDF Reports

Export detailed grading reports as PDF — includes annotated image, grade summary, per-apple breakdown, and individual crop thumbnails.

Analysis History

Every analysis is persisted in SQLite. Browse, view, re-export, or delete past results from the History page.

How it works

From image to grade in 6 steps

A fully automated pipeline — no manual intervention required.

1
📤
Upload Image

Drag & drop, browse files, or use your camera. Single image or batch of up to 20.

2
🔍
Apple Detection

YOLO26 scans the image and draws bounding boxes around every apple with confidence scores.

3
🧠
Disease Classification

Each detected crop is passed to ResNet18, which classifies it as HEALTHY, BLOTCH, ROT, or SCAB.

4
📊
Feature Extraction

Color redness, hue uniformity, and bounding box size are computed from the HSV color space.

5
🏆
Grade Assignment

A rule-based system combines defect label and color grade to assign A, B, or C quality grade.

6
📄
Export Report

Download a full PDF report with annotated image, grade summary, and per-apple details.

Quality Grades

Three-tier grading system

Each apple receives a grade based on its disease status and visual quality.

A
Premium Quality

HEALTHY Good Color

High redness score, uniform color distribution. Market-ready, top-tier fruit.

B
Standard Quality

HEALTHY fair color BLOTCH acceptable

Minor color variation or small surface blemishes. Suitable for most markets.

C
Below Standard

ROT SCAB Poor Color

Significant disease or poor color quality. Not suitable for fresh market.
